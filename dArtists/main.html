Art for the people, money for artists, glory for God. Cyrille Polissadiw, Paris 1926

D-Artists is a decentralized art project that plays on recent experiments in NFT and DeFi. Put your D-Artists NFTs to work in the Artist Studio below to earn PABLOs and redeem unique D-Art NFTs in the Exhibition Room. Keep them in your Gallery or go to the Auction Room to sell or transfer them.

The first D-Artists series (65 NFTs) and D-Art series (100 NFTs) are made by french artist Julien Silvano.D-Artists aims also to be a collective playground open for art collaboration, so artists with different backgrounds will release new series later.
